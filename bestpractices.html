What are the best practices against cyber-attacks?

The best practices against cyber-attacks follows 10 key steps, which were the following: Security of Web Development Stages, Keeping Software Updated, Installing SSL, Continuous Vulnerability Scanning and Penetration Testing, Input Sanitization and Validation, Password Security Best Practice, Monitoring Traffic Surges, Content Delivery Networks (CDNs), Implement Web Application Firewall (WAF).

What is a cyber-attack/cyber-threat?

Cyber threats and cyber-attacks refers to any possibility of an attempt of malicious intent. This can lead from a range of viruses, ransomware, phishing, and data theft, which is likely the one that most people are afraid of. Although obvious, these can be deadly to individuals, and especially organisations who seem to have lots of employees and workers, therefore increasing the chances of a cyber-attack occurring. This is the reason why understanding how you can protect yourself from these are crucial.
